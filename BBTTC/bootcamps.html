Our Bootcamps

At Best Buy Teen Tech Center, we believe that high-level tech training for underrepresented minorities is the answer to the lack of diversity in tech. It is also the path for creating more sustainable communities within the inner city. Check out our bootcamps to see which one could work for you and change your life today!
