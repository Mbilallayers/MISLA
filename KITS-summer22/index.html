Silverscreen Entertainment & Legacy Arts Connection (LAC) present Kids in the Spotlight “Script-to-Screen” in association with South Central LA’s She Ready Foundation, M.A.R.T.Y. and Made In South LA.

“Script to Screen” is a  pilot program and partnership between Silverscreen Pictures, The Legacy Arts Connection, K.I.T.S., She Ready, M.A.R.T.Y. and Made In South LA (MISLA) to prepare underserved youth and young adults, living in Los Angeles County, for entry level jobs and media arts internships in order to increase diversity in the entertainment industry. The 10-week Script-to-Screen Course pairs students with entertainment industry professionals to learn how to write a 10-page script, cast real actors, produce a short film and audition for a role in the screenplay. The finished films will be screened at their annual “KITS Film Awards” on Saturday, Nov 8, 2022.

****Students age high school and up will be considered
