How the Paid Work Experience Works?

  • Step 01

    Some participants will attend 20 hours of paid ($16.04 an hour) personal and professional development training.

    (Success = consistent attendance, promptness, class participation, coachable, ability to follow instructions and work with others)

  • Step 02

    Participants are placed with an employer for 100-320 hours of paid ($16.04 an hour) on the job work experience. (Internship will be virtual/in-person hybrid in South LA)

    • Participants can work a maximum of 30-40 hours a week

    • Participants can work 5 out of 7 days a week

    • Days available to work are Sunday - Saturday

  • Step 03

    The employer will conduct monthly performance evaluations of participants as well as complete bi-weekly time sheets

Who is Eligible for the YouthSource Program?

Youth and Young Adults Between the Ages of 14 - 30

In School (High School, College or Vocational Training)

Not in School (Graduate / Job Seeker)

Not Employed/ Must be fully vaccinated

Must Live in the City of Los Angeles

Must have the Legal Right to Work in the United States
