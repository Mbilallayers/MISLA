Facebook/Meta EFTW (Engineer For The Week)

Creating Chatbots (Game changers in the workplace)

  • Dates:

    July 5, 12, 19, 26, Aug 2, Aug 9

  • Times

    4:00pm-6:00pm

  • No. of Students:

    15 max

  • Materials:

    Internet, Facebook + Chatfuel account (only facilitator required)

  • Description:

    Youth created Chatbots, What is a Chatbot | Oracle Philippines from scratch! They named their chatbot, Ray of Sun Suicide Prevention. A group of 13- 18 year olds sketched an idea to integrate an AI (Artificial Intelligence) online bot with people who may be suicidal, in a crisis or need advice or someone to talk to. Using the program Chatfuel, along with Facebook, youth had to create a fluid conversation that would lead the person to help. They also had to organize + engineer the flow of the conversation using Chatfuel. Youth submitted their Chatbot into the Facebook Engineer For The Week competition where they can win $1,000 in prizes to give to their charity of choice.
