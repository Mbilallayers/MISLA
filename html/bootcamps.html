Our Bootcamps

At Best Buy Teen Tech Center, we believe that high-level tech training for underrepresented minorities is the answer to the lack of diversity in tech. It is also the path for creating more sustainable communities within the inner city. Check out our bootcamps to see which one could work for you and change your life today!

Music Production: Learn
Pro Tools with the Pros

Instructor: Al Rich + G Nett
Dates:

Thursdays, Oct 6 - Nov 17

Times:

4:30pm-6:30pm

No. of Students:

5 -10 students

Materials:

Computers and Pro Tools software (provided by tech center)

Description:

Participants will learn how to employ and navigate editing skills in a multi-track Pro Tools document from Grammy Award winning sound engineers. They will incorporate proper recording and mixing techniques for audio and MIDI files in the Pro Tools environment, analyze and interpret session file structures, as well as, recognize and navigate the user interface and tool set. Participants will learn how to run a session.
