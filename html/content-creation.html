How to create Tik-Tok & Instagram Reels to grow your followers


Over the last two years after experiencing a global pandemic social media has become the main source of communication across the world; covering a variety of topics and shaping pop culture, entertainment, news, and political views at your fingertips. Tik Tok and Instagram reels are the new phenomenon grabbing the attention of global ‘followers’ through engaging, entertaining, and educational content we love
In this comprehensive bootcamp your host Miesha Robinson, reveals the secrets of creating and editing Instagram and Tik-Tok content in an eye catching and professional way

Who is this for?

  • Entertainers

  • Entrepreneurs
  • Bloggers
  • Affiliate marketers
