Description

Interested in design, 2D and 3D modeling, the metaverse, or engineering? Ever wondered how your favorite AR lenses on Snap work? Want to create your own Snapchat lens to share with your friends?

Now you have the opportunity to dive deeper. The Snap Augmented Reality Lens Challenge (ARLC) is a free three-week virtual program that lets you:

✦ Learn how to build a Snapchat lens from scratch. Participate in three weeks of online tutorials and design exercises with guided support.

✦ Meet with Official Snap Lens Network Creators. Attend exclusive virtual workshops with people who design AR lenses for a living (and get paid to do it!).

✦ Level up your design chops in the final project showcase, and win prizes while you’re at it. Everyone who completes the challenge will walk away with a fully functional Snap lens, but we’ll also be offering select prizes to winners of our final project showcase:


  • cash prizes
  • a professional development opportunity to design a Lens for a client
  • an invitation to apply to the Snap Lens Academy, Snap Inc’s paid summer program for rising AR design students in community college.


All students ages 13 years or older are welcome to participate regardless of previous experience. Students with digital design experience are especially encouraged.

Registration is open on March 14, 2022. Click here to apply or fill the below form!

Estimated time commitment: Two hours per week. All students are welcome to participate regardless of previous experience; students with digital design or coding experience are especially encouraged.
